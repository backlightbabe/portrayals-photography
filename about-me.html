“Take care of all your memories, for you cannot relive them.”

—Bob Dylan


“Memories need to be saved, shared, and cherished.”

When hurricane warnings sent us scrambling to evacuate our Houston home many years ago, guess what I grabbed first? Yup, my precious stash of family photos—because some things are just irreplaceable.

Seven years deep in portrait photography, I specialize in professional headshots, family and senior portraits, and fine art images. As an artist, I understand the power of a single photograph to tell a story; as a mom, I understand the value of preserving memories. My passion for photography stems from my desire to help people bring their history to life for current and future generations to cherish.

With a keen eye for detail and an ability to make my clients feel at ease, I take pride in delivering exceptional customer service and creating a comfortable and enjoyable experience for all those who step in front of my camera. Whether you're looking for a professional headshot, a branding portrait, a traditional family portrait, or a fine art piece, I will work closely with you to bring your vision to life, whether that's creating a headshot to express your brand; a beautiful, frameable portrait to share with family and friends; or piece of art that you and your family will cherish for years or even generations to come.

I'd love to help you capture your timeless moments.
